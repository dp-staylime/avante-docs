# A variant shows "Unavailable" but the product is in stock

"Unavailable" does not mean sold out.

It means that exact option combination does not exist in your product setup.

Go to **Shopify Admin → Products → \[product] → Variants** and confirm that every option combination you want to sell is created and has inventory assigned.

Also check your market.

If you test from a country outside your store's shipping zones, Shopify can show the product as unavailable.
