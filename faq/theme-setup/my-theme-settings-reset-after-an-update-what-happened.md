# My theme settings reset after an update — what happened?

This usually happens after a major theme update.

Minor updates usually keep your Theme Editor settings.

Major updates can reset them because the theme structure changes.

### Major vs. minor updates

* **Minor or patch updates** usually transfer your visual settings automatically.
* **Major updates** can reset colors, fonts, section layouts, and block settings.

### What does not reset

Your Shopify Admin content stays safe, including:

* Products
* Collections
* Pages, blog posts, and menus

### What to do next

After a major update, reconfigure your Theme Editor settings manually to match your previous design.

For the full update process, see [Update Avante theme](../../getting-started/update-avante-theme.md).
